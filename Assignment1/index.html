Main Menu

Steak frites

Steak frites is a very common and popular dish served in brasseries throughout Europe consisting of steak paired with French fries.

Duck confit

Duck confit is a French dish made with the whole duck. In Gascony, according to the families perpetuating the tradition of duck confit, all the pieces of duck are used to produce the meal.

Chicken Marengo

Chicken Marengo is a French dish consisting of a chicken sautéed in oil with garlic and tomato, garnished with fried eggs and crayfish.
